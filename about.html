Venue Overview

Elements@Church is both a restaurant and entertainment venue, updated from its previous setup with a more spacious and modern interior. In the evening, it functions like a nightclub featuring a dance floor and lively entertainment. . The venue is known for having clean facilities, including well-maintained restrooms, and large screens for watching games or events.

Food and drinks

Offers a variety of meals such as wings and chips, grilled fish with sauces, traditional South African dishes like mogodu, and breakfast options.The food is praised for freshness and flavor, although occasional service inconsistencies with portioning and side dishes have been reported.

Dining Experience

At Elements@Church, we believe dining is more than just a meal but an experience.Nestled in the heart of Pretoria, our trendy bar and dining destination offers a unique culinary excellence and warm hospitality. We are dedicated to offering our guests an unforgettable dining journey, blending impeccable service with innovative cuisines.

We were born from a passion for exceptional food and a desire to create a space where people can come together to share great meals and memorable moments.

Our menu is a celebration of diverse flavours, crafted using the finest seasonal ingredients. We are committed to sourcing locally and sustainably, ensuring that every dish we serve is not only delicious but also supports our community.

At Elements@Church, we are more than just a restaurant with ordinary dining experience. We invite you to join us and discover the perfect blend of culinary excellence and warm hospitality that defines who we are. We are a destination for those who are seeking the finest culnary cuisines.
